Regicide Rumble VI

July 9 'til July 13 2025

Regicide Rumble is back better than ever! For the sixth time, players of all skill levels will battle on a variety of different maps, carefully considering if they want to win through diplomacy or kill off as many enemy kings as possible!

Just like the 5th edition, there will be 2 community days (July 9th & July 10th), 2 pro days (July 11th and July 12th) and a finals day (July 13th). There are 2 finals: One for players with the most king snipes who didnâ€™t win their game, and one grand final for all the winners from the first 4 days. The total prize pool of $4500+ is sponsored by Forte Strategists.

Format and Settings

Regicide Rumble games will be played on maps that are suited for regicide diplomacy settings. Maps will not be released until after players are placed and schedule is set. Players can win by defeating everyone, collecting all the relics for a relic victory, or building a wonder for a wonder victory.

Before the match starts, 10 civilizations are banned globally to ensure good variety between different games. After this, civilizations are picked by all players in the match in order from lowest 1v1 elo to highest 1v1 elo. In the event that a competitor has less than 10 ranked 1v1s their order is decided by moderator discretion. Like Regicide Rumble V, players will not start with a castle due to DE's "Regicide Mode" which will ensure greater balance of civilizations.

Regicide Rules

The following extra rules apply to ensure a fun, fair game for everyone:

  • Players must not resign
  • Players may not delete their king, or delete a transport ship with their own king in it
  • Allied victory is not allowed
  • Players may not receive any information from outside sources
  • Players can not have any streams open while playing the regicide rumble game

Admins hold the final decision on individual situations. Not following these rules may lead to a (partial) prize pool deduction.

Schedule & Prize Money

Two games are played each day of Regicide Rumble VI. Game 2 will start about 20 minutes after game 1 is finished, so we ask players to keep an eye on the first game of the day to ensure they are ready to go.

  • Game 1 - 16 GMT / 12pm EST
  • Game 2 - 18:30 GMT / 14:30 EST (Approximately)

Players will get $25 per king snipe. In a case where it is unclear which player is responsible for sniping a king, admins have the final decision. Placements are based on the time survived in the game. In case a player wins with a relic or wonder victory, 2nd and 3rd place are based on the highest score of players that are still alive when the game ends.

The winners from each game will be invited to play the Grand Final. The 8 players who did not win their game with the most kingsnipes will be invited for the kingsnipe final. In case of a tie, T90 and Rbase96 will decide which players will be invited. The kingsnipe final is the first game on July 13, the Grand Final is the second and final game.

The placement prizes are as follows:

Games 1st 2nd 3rd
Community days $100 $50 $25
Pro days $200 $125 $75
Kingsnipe final $100 $50 $25
Grand final $250 $125 $75

Additionally, there are two extra prizes:

King snipes $25 per king
Fan favorite (1 per game) $25 per game

Players will receive their prize money through PayPal after the tournament is finished. More details will be posted in the Regicide Rumble Discord when the event is finished.

Streaming & Broadcasting

Regicide Rumble VI is open streaming. Casters must have the logo on screen at all times during the game, and have a command that indicates that Regicide Rumble VI is hosted by T90Official. The logo can be found in the Regicide Rumble Discord.

POV Streaming is allowed under the following circumstances:

  • Players must have text to speech alerts removed
  • Players must have stream chat off during gameplay
  • Players must put a 2 minute stream delay within OBS

Failing these rules will lead to a prize pool deduction.

Signups

Players for Pro days and the final day must join the Regicide Rumble Discord. When you are there you will be assigned a role that will give you access to the room you'll need for setting up your game & talking to fellow players and admins. Players may also be asked to join the voice channel post match, so please be prepared for a possible interview after the game.

For Community days, players will be drawn live on stream just before the game starts in T90â€™s discord. There will also be a few community game legends rigged into each game.

For the Regicide Rumble VI Pro days, players can sign up here. The players with the highest 1v1 elo will be selected. There will also be a few special guests for the pro days, who will be announced close to the start of the event. Signups close July 4th, 23:59 GMT.
